# 🧠 AI News Intelligence System

A structured AI-powered news processing system that collects, categorizes, and summarizes updates across advanced AI domains including:

- Global AI developments  
- Recursive Self-Improvement (RSI)  
- Quantum AI research  
- Emerging AI architectures  

---

## 🚀 Overview

This system transforms raw AI-related information into a **structured intelligence feed**, organized into meaningful conceptual categories instead of generic news summaries.

It is designed to help track fast-moving AI research in a **digestible and layered format**.

---

## 🧩 Core Features

- 🗞️ Multi-category AI news classification
- 🧠 Domain-specific summarization pipelines
- 📊 Structured output formatting (section-based intelligence feed)
- ⚡ Fast processing of evolving AI topics
- 🔎 Focused tracking of advanced AI research areas

---

## 📚 Content Domains

The system organizes information into:

- 🌍 **Global AI** → industry updates and model releases  
- 🔁 **RSI (Recursive Self-Improvement)** → self-improving system research  
- ⚛️ **Quantum AI** → quantum computing + AI intersection  
- 🧬 **Emerging Architectures** → new model designs and paradigms  

---

## 🧠 Design Philosophy

Most news systems:
- Dump unstructured summaries
- Mix unrelated topics
- Lose conceptual clarity

This system instead focuses on:
- Structured intelligence layering
- Domain separation
- Signal over noise
- Research-oriented framing
